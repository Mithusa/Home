BUTTER CHICKEN

INGREDIENTS

For 4 servings

  • 2 lb bonless, skinless chicken breast, cubed
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder, divided
  • 1.2 teaspoon tumeric
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 1/2 cups diced yellow onion
  • 3 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 3-inch cinnamon stick
  • 14 oz. tomato sauce
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • cooked rice for serving
  • chopped fresh cilantro, for garnish

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a large bowl, season the chicken with salt, pepper, 1 teaspoon of chill powder, and tumeric. Let it sit for 15 minutes to marinate
  2. Melt the 2 tablespoons of butter in a large pot, pour in the chicken, and let it brown. once it browns, remove it from the pot
  3. Then add another tablespoons of butter to the pot, let it melt, and add the onions, garam masala, the remains of the chili powder, cumin, ginger, garlic, cayenne, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer after adding the tomato sauce
  5. add the water and cream
  6. After creating the mixture, add in the chicken and let it cook for about 10-15 minutes.
  7. Stir in 2 tablespoons of butter and season with salt and pepper for more taste
  8. Serve the chicken over rice and garnish with cilantro

MILK POWDER BURFI

INGREDIENTS


  • 2 1/2 cup milk powder, full cream
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup ghee/clarified butter
  • 3 tbsp. chopped pistachios

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a bowl, pour in the milk powder, the sugar, and the milk, and whisk until everything is fully incorporated
  2. Transfer the mixture to a non-stick pan, add 1/2 cup of ghee and mix on low heat
  3. Once the butter has melted, add the strawberry powder and the salt and continue mixing
  4. The mixture should start to thicken after 10 minutes. To test you can run your spoon through the middle without it running back together
  5. Keep mixing until the mixture thickens
  6. Keep mixing until the mixture thickens
  7. It will start to hold shape and separate from the pan after 10 minutes.(Remember not to overcook, as it will turn hard, or if the imxture is undercooked, it will turn chewy)
  8. Press gently and spread it evenly into a rectangle, and its leveled up
  9. Garnish with chopped pistachios
  10. Cover the dish and refrigerate for 1 hour or until it sets completely
  11. After an hour, take the dish and cut it into square with a sharp knife, and you're ready to serve! Enjoy!
