MASHED POTATOES

INGREDIENTS

For 10 servings

  • Cold water
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt, plus more to taste
  • 5 lb. russet potato, peeled and cut into large chunks
  • 8 tablespoons unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chives, chopped, for garnish

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a large pot, place the potatoes, cover with cold water, and add some salt. Bring to a boil over high heat, and cook until the potatoes are forktender, about 20 mintutes longer.
  2. Drain the potatoes, and press through a potato ricer into a bowl, and set aside
  3. Heat the butter, cream, and milk in a small pot over low heat until simmering
  4. Pour the cream mixture over the potatoes, and stir until fully combined. Season with salt and pepper, as well as garnish with chives if desired
  5. Enjoy!

Mississippi roast

INGREDIENTS

For 8 servings

  • 1 boneless chuck roast(1.3kg)
  • 1/4 cup flour (30g)
  • pepper, to taste
  • 3 tablespoons of canola oil
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 10 pepperoncini
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • fresh parsley, for garnish

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Mix the flour, salt,and pepper and rub it into the chuck roast.
  2. To form a crust, use canola oil to brown the meat on both side in a frying pan over extremely high heat
  3. Place the meat in a slow cooker and sprinkle pepperoncini and butter on top
  4. Turn the slow cooker down to low and cover it
  5. In a separate bowl, thoroughly combine the mayonnaise, vinegar, paprika, and dill
  6. After covering the meat with the mixture, simmer it on low for about eight hours
  7. Using two forks, remove the roast and shred it
  8. Return the meat to the slow cooker and stir to mix in the juices
  9. Serve with fresh parsley and enjoy!
