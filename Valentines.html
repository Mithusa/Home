CAKEPOPS

INGREDIENTS

For 24 servings

  • 15-ounce yellow cake mix(prepare according to instructions on the box
  • 1/2 cup vanilla frosting
  • 2 cups white chocolate, plus 2 tablespoons melted, divided
  • sprinkles for decorations
  • Lollipop sticks

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a large bowl, crumble the cake into crumbs and add the vanilla frosting. Combine until a dough is formed
  2. With the dough from the little balls, about 1 inch in diameter
  3. Using a cakepop stand, take a lollipop stick and dip it in melted chocolate, then press it into one of the balls.Repeat this process until everything is completed
  4. Refrigerate for an hour
  5. Take out the cake pops from the freezer, then, with your melted chocolate(it can be dyed to your liking, but for this cake, pink or red), dip one of the sticks wit the cake pop ball until the ball is fully covered. Repeat this process until all of the cake pops are covered.
  6. If you want, you can add some red or pink sprinkles.
  7. Put the cake pops back into the freezer for about an hour.
  8. Take the cake pops out and enjoy!

FINAL PRODUCT

STRAWBERRY BRIGADEIRO

INGREDIENTS


  • 1 14 ounce can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons of strawberry milk powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • Chocolate sprinkles or granulated sugar to garnish

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Using butter, grease a plate and set it aside
  2. Over medium-low heat, pour the condensed milk and butter and the salt, and continue mixing
  3. Once the butter has melted add the strawberry powder and the salt, and continue mixing
  4. The mixture should start to thicken after 10 minutes
  5. Once thickened, pour the mixture onto the buttered plate and put it in the refrigerator for 1 hour
  6. Using a cupcake liner, set thigns up
  7. Afer an hour, take the plate outisde of the refrigerator.With wet hands, put a tablespoon of the mixture on your hands and form a small bal, repeat until you have finished the mixture
  8. In a cup, pour some pink and red sprinkes, pop in each ball at a time until fully coated, and transfer to the cupcake liners to serve
  9. ENJOY!
