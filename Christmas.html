PEPPERMINT BARK

INGREDIENTS

For 15 servings

  • 25 mini candy canes
  • 4 cups chocolate chips
  • 1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • 3 cups white chocolate chip

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. The mini candy canes should be placed in a zip-lock bag, closed, and then crushed into small pieces using a rolling pin. After that, set them aside as will be using them later
  2. Melt the chocolate chips in a different bowl and stir in 1/2 teaspoon of peppermint essence
  3. Take out a tray and place some parchment paper on it. Using a spatula, evenly distribute the melted chocolate chips
  4. Free the chocolate in the freezer for 5 minutes
  5. Melt the white hot chocolate in a seperate bowl, and pour the white chocolate onto the frozen chocolate tray
  6. Then sprinkle the crushed candy canes onto the white chocolate, and freeze for an hour
  7. Break it into pieces and enjoy!

SUGAR COOKIES

INGREDIENTS

For 24 cookies

  • 3 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar(150g)
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

INGREDIENTS FOR ICING

  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • gel food coloring
  • water
  • 1/4 cup pasteurized egg white

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a bowl, combine the flour,baking soda, and salt, and whisk it well
  2. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and sugar with an electric whisk until light and fluffy for about 5 minutes
  3. add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, and add the egg
  4. Once the egg is well incorporated, add the sour cream and vanilla extract and continue whisking until everything is well combined
  5. Place plastic wrap over the dough, and refrigerate for a minimum of 60 minutes
  6. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees Fahrenheit and place parchment paper on a baking pan
  7. After removing the dough from the freezer, roll it out to a thickness of approximately 1/2 inch
  8. Using cookie cutters, cut the dough into the appropriate shapes(we went with a christmas theme)
  9. Place the cookies on a baking sheet, allow one inch or so between each cookie
  10. The cookies should be baked for about 8 minutes or until the tops are pale and the bottoms are golden brown
  11. Place the cookies on a wire rack to cool

    12. ICING

  12. Sift the powdered sugar into a bowl with a sifter
  13. Add the salt, vanilla extract, and egg whites, and mix it in until fully incorporated with an electric mixter
  14. Divide the icing into smaller bowls, and add desired food coloring(if you want the icing to be looser, add water
  15. Decorate the cookies to your liking
